About Mick

From Interior-design to Web-design! The concept of design has always been a focal point in my life. As a kid I enjoyed taking apart anything I could get my hands on to find out what makes it tick. In high school photogrophy became my new interest, especially black and white photos. Durring college fast cars and their engineering became my new hobby. Next came interior design which sparked my interest in minimalist design. Over the last 6 years inovative web-design has become my new passion. Last but not least, Travel is always in the plans.

GA-Projects

These are a few of the projects that I have colaborated on with my teamates at the General Assembly during my Immersive Engineering academy.

GA Project

Zingo

This is the web based version of the board game where you try to match the items on your board with the items you draw. The object is to get all your items before your opponent does.

GA Project

doodsre

Real Estate site that gives sellers the platform to list and sell their home with out any Real Estate agents. We provide the seller the ability to save a great deal of money, which ca also be passed down to the buyers purchase price.
